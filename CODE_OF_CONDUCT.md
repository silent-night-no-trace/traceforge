# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Traceforge is committed to a respectful, constructive, and inclusive community.

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful in discussions and reviews
- assume good intent while giving direct technical feedback
- focus on ideas, evidence, and outcomes rather than personal attacks
- welcome contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- trolling, inflammatory comments, or bad-faith disruptions
- publishing private or sensitive information without permission
- repeated hostility after being asked to stop

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

For serious incidents, contact the project maintainers privately through the repository hosting platform.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, review threads, and any project-managed communication channels.
